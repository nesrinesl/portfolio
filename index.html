First:what is portfolio ?

A portfolio is a collection of work that showcases a person's skills and experience. It can be used to apply for jobs, promote oneself to potential clients, or simply to document one's professional development.

Portfolios can be physical or digital. Physical portfolios typically contain printed samples of work, such as resumes, cover letters, writing samples, and design sketches. Digital portfolios can contain a variety of media, such as web pages, videos, and audio files

When creating a portfolio, it is important to select work that is relevant to your goals and audience,for example,if you are applying for a job in graphic design, you would want to include samples of your best graphic design work. If you are promoting yourself to potential clients, you would want to include samples of work that is similar to the work that you would be doing for them. It is also important to organize your portfolio in a logical and easy-to-navigate way. You should include clear labels and descriptions for each item, and you should make sure that your portfolio is visually appealing.
